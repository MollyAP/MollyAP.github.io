Why it matters:

Collections matter because they really help a lot in managing groups of related objects quickly and efficiently, allowing us to store, retrieve, and manipulate data, so we can handle large amounts of information and adapt to changing application requirements easier. There are even different types of collections, like lists, dictionaries, queues, stacks etc, and each have specific use cases and advantages.
