Entity Framework and APIs

Why it matters:

This matters because it simplifies the process of interacting with databases by letting us work with database entities as regular objects in our code. It simplifies the complexities of SQL queries and provides a higher-level, object-oriented approach to database operations which reduces the amount of reused code required and lets us focus on other important things instead of database management.
