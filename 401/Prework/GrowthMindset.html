Why it matters:

Deliberate practice and upgrading technical skills matters because in the software development field, continuous improvement and staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and practices are essential not only for professional growth and career advancement but also to keep your job. Deliberate practice allows us to push ourselves outside of our comfort zones, and seek challenges that really will enhance our skills. By engaging in deliberate practice, we can overcome obstacles, learn new techniques, and improve our problem-solving abilities.
