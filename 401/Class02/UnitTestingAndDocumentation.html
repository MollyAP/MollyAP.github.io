Why it matters:

Since we're studying complicted new stuff like .NET, C#, and SQL, debugging is something that will be VERY important because it helps identify and resolve errors and bugs in our code.
unit tests let us:
  1. Verify the correctness and reliability of individual sections of code.
  2. Learning how to use xUnit.net allows us to integrate unit testing seamlessly into your .NET projects and by understanding the concepts and techniques of xUnit.net, we can write effective and efficient tests for our code.
  3. When you're working in a team, unit tests help a lot because they give a standardized and reliable way to validate changes made by different team members.
By learning how to make tests with xUnit we will begin to adopt testdriven development practices, better cooperate in team projects, and validate interactions with databases.
