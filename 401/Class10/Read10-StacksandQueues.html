Stacks and Queues - Vocabulary/Definition

  • Stack: A data structure that follows the Last In First Out (LIFO) or First In Last Out (FILO) order.
  • Push: Adding a new item to the top of the stack.
  • Pop: Removing the top item from the stack.
  • Top: The reference to the top item in the stack.
  • Peek: Viewing the value of the top item without removing it.
  • IsEmpty: A method to check if the stack is empty.
  • Queue: A data structure that follows the First In First Out (FIFO) or Last In Last Out (LILO) order.
  • Enqueue: Adding a new item to the rear of the queue.
  • Dequeue: Removing the front item from the queue.
  • Front: The reference to the front item in the queue.
  • Rear: The reference to the rear item in the queue.
