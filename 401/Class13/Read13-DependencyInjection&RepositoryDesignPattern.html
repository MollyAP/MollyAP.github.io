Why it matters:

It matters because it provides a easy structured way to manage and fulfill dependencies between various parts of my app. In the real world apps aren't built as single, large, and tightly integrated peices... instead they're made of various classes and modules that interact with each other. They facilitate the management of these interactions by decoupling components and allowing them to be independently developed, tested, and replaced. This results in more modular, maintainable, and testable code!
