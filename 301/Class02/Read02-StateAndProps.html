React State Vs Props

  1. What types of things can you pass in the props?
    2. Any data type, objects, arrays, booleans, numbers, strings, functions, etc

  2. What is the big difference between props and state?
    3. States are internal and controlled by the component itself while props are external and controlled by whatever renders the component.
  3. When do we re-render our application?
    4. when data has been changed, the app needs to be rerendered
  4. What are some examples of things that we could store in state?
    5. Forms and strings.
