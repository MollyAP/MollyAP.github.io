Component-Based Architecture

  1. What is a “component”?
    2. A component is a modular and self-contained software object that encapsulates a specific functionality or set of functionalities, and is designed to interact with other components through well-defined interfaces

  2. What are the characteristics of a component?
    3. Reusability, Replaceablity, Non specific, Extensibility, Encapsubility, and Independibility!

  3. What are the advantages of using component-based architecture?
    4. Easy to deploy and develop, it's reusable and reliable, and costs less to develop and maintain
